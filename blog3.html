SAVE TREES, SAVE LIFES !!

A mature leafy tree produces as much oxygen in a season as 10 people inhale in a year. They clean up our pollution, carbons, they store and purify water, and they gives us medicine that cures ours diseases, they give foods that feeds us.

We are cutting them down day by day. We don’t realize how special our earth is. Tress has also the right to live same as us. We just cut the trees down for money. Yes, today we live in the world where cutting down trees makes us money. That money could get us happiness for a couple of days, but we don’t realize while cutting trees, that tree would make us happiness for rest of our life. Cutting down trees takes out oxygen, tress are even necessary for rain and cutting down trees at the larger extent, it will definitely affect the rainfall. When we cut down trees, we are taking the home away from animals because it’s their only home where they could stay at.

The great sorrow is most of us today, we don’t care about tomorrow. We don’t realize what we have until it is gone. We people use nature as a credit card with no spending limit on it. We can ignore this but the thing about truth is; it can be denied, not avoided. We are too caught up in our doings to do something, we listen to the people who makes excuses to do nothing. So we don’t have to wait for the politicians or government to take care of our nature. We are not apart from nature, we are a part of nature, to betray nature is to betray us and to save the nature is to save us.

We live in the world where cutting down trees make us money, but a wise man once said, when the rivers all dried up and the trees all cut down, men will then realize that he will not able to eat money.
